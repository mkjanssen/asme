Definition 2.1.10.

The Sum of Squares for the Model (SSModel) measures the variation in the group means from the overall mean.

\begin{equation*} SSModel = \sum\limits_{\text{all obs}} (\text{group mean} - \text{overall mean})^2 = \sum\limits_{\text{all groups}} (\text{group size})\times (\text{effect})^2. \end{equation*}
